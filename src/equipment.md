# Equipment

## Required by Law

- Life Jackets
- Navigation Lights (night use only)
- Sound Signaling Devices
- Visual Distress Signals (Coastal Waters Only)
- Throwable Type IV Life Preserver (boat cushion)

## Recommended

- Cellphone
- VHF Radio
- Sunscreen
- Foul Weather Jacket
- First Aid Kit
- Water
- Sweet Snack
- Knife
- Spare light line
- Flashlight
